Other Options

Any other camera that has ether RTMP, RTMPS, STP, would work in to OBS or Live Stream, If the camera have an HDMI out you will need a HDMI Capture Card for them. (Elgato Cam Link 4k $238 )

Sound

For events in the Audi it is best the use a mic and run the sound through the desk, It would pay if the Audi replace the condenser/shotgun mics that have gone missing which are good for picking up groups of people not just one person, good for house drama and music. The mic in the MEVO camera is ok up close but further than 5-10 m then it pays to have a separate mic, which just needs to be a mic that goes to 3.5mm so you can use the MEVO camera mic app.
